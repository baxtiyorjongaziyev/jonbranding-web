## 2026-06-15 - Focus Visible on Mobile Navigation
**Learning:** Mobile menu triggers and internal buttons (like 'Close Menu' or CTA) often lack keyboard focus indicators because they are primarily designed for touch interactions. However, keyboard users (including those using alternative input devices or screen readers on mobile devices) rely on visible focus to navigate.
**Action:** Always verify that mobile-specific interactive elements (like hamburger menus, off-canvas close buttons, and inner quick actions) include explicit 'focus-visible' utility classes.
