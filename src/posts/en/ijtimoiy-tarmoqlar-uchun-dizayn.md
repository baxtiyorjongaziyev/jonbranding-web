## Social media is your digital storefront

Today, most customers first get acquainted with your business through social media. Your Instagram, Facebook, or Telegram page is your virtual office, store, and the face of your brand.

Now imagine you walk into an expensive boutique, but inside it's a mess, the shelves are different colors, the lighting doesn't match... You'd want to leave such a place as soon as possible, right?

A social media page consisting of mismatched posts with different fonts and colors leaves the same impression. It undermines the professionalism and credibility of the brand.

## The hidden danger of template designs

Many businesses use ready-made templates from platforms like "Canva" to save time and money. This may seem like an easy and quick solution, but it has several major drawbacks:

*   **Lack of uniqueness:** The template you use is used by thousands of other people. This causes your brand to dissolve into the crowd and not stand out from competitors.
*   **Inconsistency with strategy:** Templates do not take into account your brand strategy, its voice, and character. They are just "pretty pictures."
*   **Limited possibilities:** Templates limit your creative freedom and do not allow you to fully express your brand.
