## Why is a brand name so important?

A brand name is a central part of your marketing and communication strategy. It's your domain name, your social media pages, and most importantly, how your customers will remember you. A well-chosen name can give your business wings, while a bad one can hinder its development.

## 7 rules for finding the perfect name:

1.  **Be memorable:** The name should be easy to pronounce and write. If people can't remember your name or can't spell it correctly in a search, they won't find you. Examples: *Google, Apple, Nike.*
2.  **Carry meaning:** The name should say something about your activity, values, or offering. This can be direct (e.g., *Tashkent Furniture*) or indirect (e.g., *Amazon* - huge selection).
3.  **Be unique and stand out:** Avoid names similar to your competitors. Your goal is to stand out, not get lost. Before choosing a name, check if there are competitors with the same name.
4.  **Be flexible and expandable:** Don't limit yourself to the name of one product or place. If you plan to expand your business in the future, your name should allow for it. For example, the name *"Tashkent Burger"* is limited to Tashkent and only burgers.
5.  **Ensure available domains and social media handles:** Before choosing a name, check if '.uz' and '.com' domains are available, and also check if accounts with that name are not taken on Instagram, Telegram, and other social networks.
6.  **Be legally clean:** Make sure the name you choose is not registered as a trademark by another company. Otherwise, you may face serious legal problems in the future.
7.  **Evoke positive associations:** The name should evoke emotions and images that are consistent with your goals. Also, check if it has a negative meaning in other languages.

Finding a good name takes time and research. Take this process seriously, because it is one of the most important bricks in the foundation of your brand.
