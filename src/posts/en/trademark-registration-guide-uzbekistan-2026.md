## What is a Trademark and Why Do You Need One?

A **Trademark** is a sign (name, logo, or combination of colors) that distinguishes your goods or services from competitors. Registering it gives you:

1. **Exclusive rights:** No one else can legally use your name.
2. **Legal protection:** The right to take competitors to court if they steal your brand.
3. **Franchise potential:** The ability to sell or license your brand to others.
4. **Market trust:** Customers trust officially registered brands far more.

## Registration Steps

In Uzbekistan, the process typically follows this order:

### Step 1: Expert Check
Before submitting documents, you need to verify that your brand is not similar to existing ones. This is also called **Step 0**.

### Step 2: Filing the Application
The application is submitted to the Intellectual Property Center under the Ministry of Justice. The first state fee is paid at this stage.

### Step 3: State Examination
Specialists examine your brand for 7–9 months (standard procedure). If you need it faster, there is an **Accelerated procedure (10–20 days)**.
