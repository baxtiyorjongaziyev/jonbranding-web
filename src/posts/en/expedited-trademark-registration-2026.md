Imagine: you are launching a new product and urgently need to register your brand within three weeks. Waiting the standard 7–9 months is simply not an option. That's where the **Expedited Procedure** comes in.

## What is Expedited Trademark Registration?

In Uzbekistan, there are two procedures for trademark registration:

| | Standard Procedure | Expedited Procedure |
|---|---|---|
| **Timeline** | 7–9 months | 10–20 days |
| **Agency Service** | 5,000,000 UZS | 7,000,000 UZS |
| **Best for** | When time allows | Urgent situations |

## How Does the Expedited Procedure Work?

In the expedited procedure, documents are processed **with priority** — government experts review your application ahead of the queue, bypassing other applications. This is why the cost is slightly higher.

The process goes as follows:
1. **Day 1:** Document preparation and application filing.
2. **Days 2–5:** Technical examination conducted on our side.
3. **Days 5–20:** Accelerated state review.
4. **Day 20:** Decision on issuing the certificate or rejection.

## Who is the Expedited Procedure Ideal For?

- **Startups:** When you need to protect your brand before an investor meeting.
- **Franchisors:** To secure rights before a new partner enters the market.
- **Competitive threats:** When a competitor is about to register your exact name.
- **Marketing campaigns:** For legally protecting the brand before a major advertising launch.

## Important Note

> The expedited procedure does not provide a 100% guarantee — if your brand is similar to another trademark, it may be rejected. This is why a **preliminary examination (Step 0)** is critically important.
